# Work Strand: Vision Alignment & Transformation Readiness

**ID:** WS-002
**Status:** Active
**Created:** 2026-01-26
**Owner:** Ben Smoot

---

## Purpose

Align leaders and teams to the future vision of a tech-enabled sales execution platform to drive increased revenue and improved customer and UPsecure experience. Establish the foundation for successful transformation through clarity, accountability, and tight feedback loops.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Vision definition, vetting, and communication
- Landscape assessment (cultural, tactical, awareness)
- Stakeholder alignment and accountability agreements
- Operating principles and cornerstones
- Future state definition and prioritization
- AI/human role clarity
- Use case cataloging and impact mapping
- Prototype-evaluate-position-plan cycles
- Full execution lifecycle with measurement

### Out of Scope (for now)
- Technical implementation decisions (handled by WS-001)
- Budget approvals and procurement
- HR/org structure changes
