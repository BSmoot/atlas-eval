## Key Processes and Frameworks

### The Gateway

UPSTACK's operating governance system for prioritization and decision-making.

**Version:** 2.0 launching Q4 2025, testing Q1 2026

**Function:** Evaluate and prioritize all incremental program/project requests

**Governance:** VP and ELT groups

### Domain Reviews

Data governance process for reviewing data quality by business domain.

**Owner:** Program 04 (Moe)

**Cadence:** Regular reviews using Salesforce data quality reports

**Output:** Issues identified, resolution tracking, quality scoring

### Insight-to-Action Mechanism

Closed-loop process connecting analysis to prioritized action.

**Programs:** 01, 02, 03

**Elements:**
- Insights surfaced from data
- Prioritized action based on insights
- Results measured and fed back
- Continuous loop, not periodic

## Key Metrics Vocabulary

### Revenue Metrics

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **MRR** | Monthly Recurring Revenue — the standard for bookings measurement in 2026 (replacing MRC) |
| **MRC** | Monthly Recurring Charges — legacy forecasting basis, converting to MRR in Q1 |
| **PF LQA** | Pro Forma Last Quarter Annualized — revenue growth measurement |
| **Pipeline Coverage** | Ratio of pipeline value to revenue target (target: 3x) |
| **Bookings** | New MRR created in a period |

### Operational Metrics

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Ramp Time** | Time for new sales hire to achieve productivity targets |
| **Cost-to-Support** | CX operational cost measurement (methodology TBD Q1) |
| **Data Quality Score** | Weighted/scored assessment of data accuracy and completeness |

### Customer Metrics

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **CSAT** | Customer Satisfaction Score |
| **Customer Health Score** | Composite indicator of account health (Lumopath) |
| **Customer Engagement Score** | Measure of customer interaction patterns |
| **Renewal Rate** | Percentage of revenue retained at renewal |
