## Who We Are

A 260-person IT Infrastructure channel business that brokers transactions between enterprises of all industries (mid to upper market) and infrastructure service providers.

## Core Services

1. Data Center Colocation
2. Network Connectivity
3. CCaaS (Contact Center as a Service)
4. UCaaS (Unified Communications as a Service)
5. CX/AI Tooling
6. Security
7. Managed Services
8. IoT

**Bread and butter:** Network connectivity and data center.

## Value Proposition

- Act as **advisor** and **execution support** for the customer
- **Aligned with customer's best interests**
- **Simplify** procurement and management lifecycle across myriad suppliers
- Deliver **insights and expert guidance** for better business decisions

## Current Strategic Focus

**Growth model:** Acquisition of smaller companies + organic hiring

**2026 Focus:**
1. Prove ability to drive **organic cross-sell** and **new logo sales** through demand generation and sales professional investments
2. Transform into a **scalable, repeatable, predictable execution platform** with:
   - Real data management
   - Proprietary software
   - Insights-driven decision making

---

## Governing Principles

*To be captured as the system evolves. Principles should reflect UPSTACK's values and constraints.*

1. **Customer alignment first** — Advice and execution always serve customer interests
2. **Simplification** — Reduce complexity for customers across supplier relationships
3. **Insight-driven** — Decisions backed by data and expertise
4. **Scalable execution** — Build repeatable processes, not one-offs

---

## Constraints

- Local-first data storage (security requirement)
- No automated external communication without human review
- No deletion or destruction of information
- No external data leakage
