## Purpose

Enable data-driven decision-making across the organization through mature governance, reliable measurement, and trusted reporting.

## Scope

**In scope:**
- Reporting overhaul (P0/P1/P2 metrics-that-matter)
- Domain reporting ownership and adoption
- Self-service analytics enablement
- Data ownership and accountability model
- Data quality scoring and historical cleanup
- Governance framework (policy, education, enforcement)

**Out of scope:**
- Application development outside data platform
