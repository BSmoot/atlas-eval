## Confirmed Deliverable Scope

### Core (Aligned)
- Content creation/infrastructure is comprehensive
- New revenue and marketing automation tools are integrated
- Playbooks with talk tracks and objection handling are included

### Additional Confirmed Deliverables
- Measurement of what content gets used and what correlates with wins
- Orchestration and systematic feedback loops between Marketing/Sales/BDRs/CX and between Strategic Insights/Data and M/S/B/C
- Ownership and updating of collateral, training, etc.
- Data quality enforcement
- Centralized intake for customer engagement inputs (customer advisory board, marketing/product interviews, CX/seller conversations, etc.)

### Sales Enablement Detail Plan - Additional Scope
- Diagnosis of what reps need most today for prioritization
- Ramp time impact focus - onboarding audit key to defining this
- Stakeholders for Marketing/CX/Insights/Data included; knowledge transfer between reps/teams on winning messaging/strategies and challenges/triggers/execution problems
- Structuring buyer journey by stage with specific content aligned to need at each stage
- Connecting content usage to bookings - which assets correlate with wins
- BANT support to be evaluated for inclusion
- Coordination of proposal/quote/audit automation with technical capabilities of portal and AI systems
